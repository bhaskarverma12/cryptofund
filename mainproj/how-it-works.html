How It Works

Bringing your ideas to life is simple, transparent, and secure.

For Creators

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Start by connecting your Ethereum wallet (like MetaMask). This is your secure identity on our platform.
  2. Create Your Campaign: Click "Create Campaign" and fill out the details: your project title, story, funding goal, and duration.
  3. Share Your Vision: Share your campaign page with your community and across social media to attract backers.
  4. Receive Funds: Once your campaign deadline is reached and the goal is met, the funds are transferred directly to your wallet.

For Backers

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Securely connect your wallet to browse and back campaigns.
  2. Discover Projects: Explore live campaigns on our homepage. Click on any project to learn more about it.
  3. Fund a Campaign: Found a project you love? Enter the amount of ETH you wish to contribute and confirm the transaction in your wallet.
  4. Track Your Support: Visit your "My Profile" page to see a history of all the innovative projects you've helped bring to life.
